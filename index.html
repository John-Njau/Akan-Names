About the Akan

The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living primarily in the countries of present-day Ghana and Ivory Coast.

Akan Naming System

In Akan culture, the newborn are given their first name with regards to the day of the week in which they were born and in respect to their gender. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.
